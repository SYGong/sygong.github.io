## Concept
A measure is a generalization of concepts of length, area, volume.

## Details
Let $$\Sigma$$ on the *set* $$X$$.

$$\mu: \Sigma \mapsto \overline \R$$ is a measure on $$X$$.

Where $$\overline \R = \R \cup \{-\infty, +\infty\}$$.
